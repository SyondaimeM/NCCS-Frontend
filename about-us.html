About Us

Empowering the Nepalese Canadian community through culture, service, and unity since our inception.

Who We Are

Building Bridges, Preserving Heritage

The Nepalese Canadian Community Services (NCCS) is a non-profit organization dedicated to serving the Nepalese Canadian community and promoting Nepali culture, traditions, and values in Canada.

We strive to create a vibrant, inclusive community where members can connect, celebrate their heritage, and support one another. Through cultural events, educational programs, and community services, we work to preserve our rich traditions while embracing the opportunities of our new home.

Our mission is to empower individuals and families by providing resources, fostering connections, and creating opportunities for personal and professional growth. Together, we are building a stronger, more united community.

Message from the President

Sudarshan Sapkota

President, NCCS

Dear Community Members and Friends,

Namaskar!

It is my great honor and privilege to serve as the President of Nepalese Canadian Community Services (NCCS). Since its founding, NCCS has been a pillar for the Nepali diaspora in Canada promoting unity, preserving our rich culture, and supporting the Nepalese community.

Our central mission is to provide essential services and strengthen our community through focused programs in education, social engagement, and cultural preservation that bring people together.

As we move forward, NCCS will continue to champion the values of inclusiveness, collaboration, and service. Together, we are committed to building a stronger, more connected Nepali-Canadian community one that contributes positively to the broader Canadian society while proudly carrying our Nepali identity.

I warmly encourage everyone to get involved, volunteer, and actively take part in NCCS initiatives. Your participation, creative ideas, and unwavering enthusiasm are the vital forces that make our community vibrant and meaningful.

Thank you for your continued support and trust. Let us work together to make NCCS a true home away from home for every Nepali in Canada.

Warm regards,
Sudarshan Sapkota
President

Our Mission

To empower and unite the Nepalese Canadian community by preserving our cultural heritage, providing essential services, and fostering opportunities for personal and professional growth. We are committed to building bridges between generations and creating a supportive environment where all members can thrive.

Our Vision

To be the leading organization that celebrates and strengthens the Nepalese Canadian identity, creating a vibrant, inclusive community where cultural traditions are honored, and every member has the resources and support needed to succeed in Canada while maintaining strong ties to our Nepali roots.
