Become a Member

Join the Nepalese Canadian Community Services and be part of a vibrant community dedicated to preserving culture and creating opportunities.

Why Become a Member?

As a member of NCCS, you'll have access to exclusive events, networking opportunities, and a supportive community that shares your values and heritage.

Join us in preserving Nepali culture, supporting community initiatives, and building lasting connections with fellow members across Canada.

Your membership helps fund programs, events, and services that benefit the entire Nepalese Canadian community.
